FAQ

Each game has a collection of 500 nfts that reward holders with royalties, which come from in-game play fees. To play a game the user has to pay a small fixed fee that is usually equivalent to 4% of the capital at stake. 75% of these fees get distributed between the nft holders of that given game.

Mint price is 2 SOL for the Masterchip NFT.

75% - NFT Holders
20% - House Wallet
5% - DG Team
Update: Until the game 2 release, 100% of fees go to holders + 100% royalty fees from secondary.

Initial Liquidity per game: 75 Sol
To ensure constant liquidity for each game, 20% of all collected fees are allocated to the House Wallet. This will make it extremely unlikely that the house wallet will ever run out of funds. There will be multiple house wallets, 1 for each game.
